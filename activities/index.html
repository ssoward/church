The Alien Hat Riddle

A Classic Logic Puzzle with an Extraterrestrial Twist

An interactive simulation of the famous hat riddle: an alien captures 10 humans and they must use a parity strategy to guarantee 9 out of 10 survive. Step through the reasoning, watch the strategy unfold, and learn about logical thinking and teamwork.

Audience: Youth, Family Home Evening, Teaching Moments
