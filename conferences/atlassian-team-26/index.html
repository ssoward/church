General Conference Tips
⏱
Standby Strategy
Arrive 5–10 minutes early for standby sessions. Rovo Theater fills fastest — be there by the time the previous session ends.
📱
Official App
Use the Team '26 app for floor maps, session reminders, and My Schedule. Add sessions tonight so you get notifications.
🎙
Keynotes
All keynotes are livestreamed, but in-person energy is worth it. Arrive early — Main Stage fills up for founder keynotes.
🤝
Networking
Welcome Reception (Tue), Rovo Happy Hour (Wed), BASH (Thu). Don't skip these — the hallway track is where deals and ideas happen.
🔄
Backup Plan
Session full? Head to Expo Theaters or peer meetups. They're less crowded and often just as good for specific demos.
🎒
Essentials
Comfortable shoes, portable charger (full battery), water bottle, and business cards or a QR code for your LinkedIn.
